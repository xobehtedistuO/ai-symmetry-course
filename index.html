Lesson 1: Bilateral Symmetry - The Perfect Mirror

Welcome to your first practical lesson! We're starting with the most intuitive and common form of symmetry: Bilateral Symmetry.

Think of a butterfly, a human face, or the Rorschach inkblot test. In each case, you can draw a line down the middle (an axis), and the left side is a mirror image of the right. This creates a powerful sense of balance, harmony, and intentional design.

In AI art, prompting for this type of symmetry is a foundational skill. It allows you to create striking portraits, iconic symbols, and balanced landscapes with ease.
